r_to_z {MAc}R Documentation

Correlation to Fisher's z

Description

Computes Fisher's z from correlation coefficient.

Usage

r_to_z(r)

Arguments

r

Correlation coefficient value.

Value

Outputs Fisher's z statistic.

Author(s)

AC Del Re & William T. Hoyt

Maintainer: AC Del Re acdelre@gmail.com
